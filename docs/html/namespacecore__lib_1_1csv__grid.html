enum core_lib::csv_grid::eCellFormatOptions
strong

Cell format options enumeration.

This enumeration is used to control the format of the cells within a row of the CSV grid, in particular whether or not they are surrounded by double qoutes or not.

Enumerator
simpleCells 

All cells are simple an not wrapped in double quotes, e.g. x1,x2,x3.

doubleQuotedCells 

Cells may contain special characters and are in double qoutes, e.g. "x1","x2","x3".

Enumerator
